Entry Point

Creator Agreement

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

This Creator Agreement ("Agreement") governs the relationship between Entry Point ("Platform," "we," "our," or "us") and content creators, influencers, and promotional partners ("Creator," "you," or "your") who wish to promote giveaways on our platform.

1. SCOPE OF AGREEMENT

This Agreement applies to all creators who:

  • Promote Entry Point giveaways on social media
  • Create content featuring our platform
  • Participate in our affiliate or partnership programs
  • Receive compensation or benefits for promotional activities

2. CREATOR OBLIGATIONS

2.1 Content Standards

  • All promotional content must be truthful and not misleading
  • Clearly disclose your relationship with Entry Point using #ad, #sponsored, or #partnership hashtags
  • Comply with FTC guidelines for endorsements and testimonials
  • Follow platform-specific disclosure requirements (Instagram, TikTok, etc.)

2.2 Prohibited Activities

  • Making false claims about odds, prizes, or platform features
  • Encouraging fraudulent activities or multiple account creation
  • Sharing inappropriate or offensive content
  • Violating any applicable laws or platform terms of service

2.3 Compliance Requirements

  • Follow all applicable advertising and consumer protection laws
  • Comply with social media platform guidelines and terms of service
  • Maintain accurate records of promotional activities
  • Respond promptly to any compliance-related inquiries

3. COMPENSATION

3.1 Commission Structure

Creators may earn commissions based on:

  • Referred user registrations
  • Entry purchases made by referred users
  • Performance bonuses for high-performing content

3.2 Payment Terms

  • Payments are made monthly for the previous month's activity
  • Minimum payout threshold of $50
  • Payments processed via PayPal, bank transfer, or other approved methods
  • Tax documentation required for payments over $600 annually

3.3 Tracking and Attribution

  • Unique referral codes or links provided for tracking
  • Attribution window of 30 days from initial click/visit
  • Creators responsible for using provided tracking methods correctly

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

4.1 Platform Content

  • Entry Point grants limited license to use our logos, images, and promotional materials
  • Usage must comply with our brand guidelines
  • No modification of provided materials without prior approval

4.2 Creator Content

  • Creators retain ownership of their original content
  • Entry Point receives non-exclusive license to share and promote creator content
  • Creators warrant they have rights to all content elements (music, images, etc.)

5. RELATIONSHIP TERMS

5.1 Independent Contractor Status

  • Creators are independent contractors, not employees
  • No exclusive relationship unless specifically agreed in writing
  • Creators responsible for their own taxes and business expenses

5.2 Performance Expectations

  • Maintain professional standards in all communications
  • Respond to platform communications within reasonable timeframes
  • Meet any agreed-upon content delivery deadlines

6. TERMINATION

6.1 Termination Rights

Either party may terminate this Agreement:

  • With 30 days written notice
  • Immediately for material breach
  • Immediately for violations of legal requirements

6.2 Post-Termination

  • Outstanding payments will be processed according to normal schedule
  • License to use Entry Point materials terminates immediately
  • Surviving provisions include intellectual property and dispute resolution clauses

7. LEGAL COMPLIANCE

7.1 FTC Compliance

Creators must comply with FTC Endorsement Guidelines, including:

  • Clear and conspicuous disclosure of material connections
  • Honest opinions based on actual experience
  • Disclosure in native language of the audience

7.2 Platform Compliance

  • Follow all social media platform advertising policies
  • Comply with age restrictions and content guidelines
  • Maintain current knowledge of platform policy changes

8. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Creators represent and warrant that:

  • They have authority to enter this Agreement
  • All content will be original or properly licensed
  • Promotional activities will comply with applicable laws
  • No conflicts of interest exist that would impair performance

9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW, ENTRY POINT'S LIABILITY TO CREATORS SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL COMPENSATION PAID TO THE CREATOR IN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

10. INDEMNIFICATION

Creators agree to indemnify Entry Point against claims arising from:

  • Breach of this Agreement
  • Violation of applicable laws or regulations
  • Infringement of third-party intellectual property rights
  • False or misleading promotional content

11. CONFIDENTIALITY

Creators agree to maintain confidentiality of:

  • Non-public platform information
  • User data or analytics shared for promotional purposes
  • Upcoming features or promotional campaigns
  • Compensation terms and performance metrics

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through:

  • Good faith negotiation between the parties
  • Binding arbitration if negotiation fails
  • Arbitration conducted under American Arbitration Association rules

13. MODIFICATIONS

Entry Point may modify this Agreement with 30 days notice. Continued participation in the creator program constitutes acceptance of modifications.

14. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to conflict of law provisions.
