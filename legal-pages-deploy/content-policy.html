Entry Point

Content Policy

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

Entry Point is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and legal environment for all users. This Content Policy outlines the standards for acceptable content and behavior on our platform.

1. PROHIBITED CONTENT

The following types of content are strictly prohibited on Entry Point:

1.1 Illegal Content

  • Content that violates any applicable laws or regulations
  • Content that promotes illegal activities
  • Copyrighted material used without permission
  • Content that violates intellectual property rights

1.2 Harmful Content

  • Harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior
  • Content that promotes violence or self-harm
  • Hate speech targeting individuals or groups
  • Content that exploits or endangers minors

1.3 Inappropriate Content

  • Sexually explicit or suggestive content
  • Graphic violence or disturbing imagery
  • Content promoting dangerous activities
  • Spam, scams, or fraudulent schemes

1.4 Platform Manipulation

  • Attempts to manipulate giveaway outcomes
  • Creating multiple accounts to circumvent rules
  • Coordinated inauthentic behavior
  • Automated or bot activity

2. USER-GENERATED CONTENT

2.1 Profile Information

Users are responsible for ensuring their profile information:

  • Is accurate and truthful
  • Does not impersonate others
  • Does not contain offensive or inappropriate material
  • Complies with our community standards

2.2 Comments and Messages

All user communications must:

  • Be respectful and constructive
  • Avoid profanity or offensive language
  • Not contain personal attacks or harassment
  • Respect other users' privacy and dignity

2.3 Contest-Related Content

When participating in giveaways, users must:

  • Follow all specific contest rules and requirements
  • Provide accurate information for prize fulfillment
  • Not attempt to manipulate or game the system
  • Respect the rights of other participants

3. CONTENT MODERATION

3.1 Automated Detection

We use automated systems to detect and remove:

  • Spam and repetitive content
  • Potentially harmful or offensive material
  • Copyright-infringing content
  • Suspicious account activity

3.2 Human Review

Our moderation team reviews content reported by users and flagged by automated systems. We aim to review all reports within 24-48 hours.

3.3 Appeals Process

Users may appeal content moderation decisions by:

  • Contacting our support team at support@entrypointapp.com
  • Providing clear explanation of why the action was incorrect
  • Submitting any relevant evidence or context
  • Following our appeals process guidelines

4. REPORTING VIOLATIONS

4.1 How to Report

Users can report policy violations through:

  • In-app reporting tools
  • Email to abuse@entrypointapp.com
  • Contact form on our website
  • Direct message to our official social media accounts

4.2 What to Include

When reporting violations, please provide:

  • Specific description of the violation
  • Screenshots or other evidence
  • Username or account information of the violator
  • Any relevant context or additional information

4.3 Response Timeline

We commit to:

  • Acknowledging reports within 24 hours
  • Initial review within 48 hours
  • Resolution or status update within 5 business days
  • Regular updates for complex investigations

5. ENFORCEMENT ACTIONS

5.1 Warning System

First-time violations may result in:

  • Educational notification about policy violation
  • Temporary restriction of certain features
  • Required acknowledgment of community guidelines
  • Monitoring of future activity

5.2 Temporary Restrictions

Repeated or serious violations may result in:

  • Temporary suspension of account access
  • Removal of specific content or privileges
  • Restriction from participating in giveaways
  • Limited ability to interact with other users

5.3 Permanent Bans

Severe violations may result in:

  • Permanent account termination
  • Prohibition from creating new accounts
  • Removal from all Entry Point services
  • Reporting to law enforcement if applicable

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1 Copyright Protection

We respect intellectual property rights and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). Users must not:

  • Upload copyrighted content without permission
  • Use trademarks without authorization
  • Claim ownership of others' intellectual property
  • Distribute pirated or stolen content

6.2 DMCA Process

Copyright holders may submit takedown requests by providing:

  • Identification of copyrighted work
  • Location of infringing content on our platform
  • Contact information and authorization to act
  • Good faith statement of infringement

6.3 Counter-Notifications

Users may submit counter-notifications if they believe content was removed in error, following DMCA guidelines for counter-notice procedures.

7. PRIVACY AND DATA PROTECTION

7.1 Personal Information

Users must not share:

  • Other users' personal information without consent
  • Private communications or confidential information
  • Financial information or payment details
  • Any information that could compromise user safety

7.2 Data Collection

We collect and use data in accordance with our Privacy Policy, including:

  • Information necessary for platform operation
  • Data required for legal compliance
  • Analytics for improving user experience
  • Security information for fraud prevention

8. COMMERCIAL CONTENT

8.1 Promotional Guidelines

Commercial or promotional content must:

  • Be clearly identified as advertising or sponsored content
  • Comply with FTC guidelines for endorsements
  • Not make false or misleading claims
  • Follow platform-specific advertising policies

8.2 Affiliate Marketing

Users engaging in affiliate marketing must:

  • Disclose affiliate relationships clearly
  • Comply with applicable regulations
  • Not engage in deceptive practices
  • Respect other users' experience

9. POLICY UPDATES

9.1 Notification of Changes

We will notify users of policy changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email announcements
  • Website updates
  • Social media communications

9.2 Effective Date

Policy changes take effect immediately upon posting unless otherwise specified. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated policies.

10. CONTACT INFORMATION

For questions about this Content Policy, contact:

General Inquiries: support@entrypointapp.com
Policy Violations: abuse@entrypointapp.com
Copyright Issues: dmca@entrypointapp.com
Website: https://entrypointapp.com/support
